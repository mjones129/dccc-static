Life Groups

Life Groups

At Dade City Christian Church, we think life is better together. ​​The main way that we live this out is by meeting in groups together. In our Life Groups, we study along with the Sunday morning program as well as special studies that run during different seasons of the year. We cover topics like marriage, parenting, Biblical basics, and more. The best part about our groups is that it connects you to the two things (we think) you need more than anything: Jesus and relationships.

Next Steps

We believe that everyone has a purpose that comes straight from God. We also believe that each person’s purpose can be encouraged, developed, and maybe even discovered by joining God’s people in what we call church. The first place to begin discovering and developing your God-given purpose in life is at our Next Steps Class. In this class we navigate through four sections:

  1. How God sees us
  2. How God saves us
  3. How God structures us
  4. How God sends us

Ad the end of this 2 hour class, you will have a clear view of how to pursue your purpose here at Dade City Christian Church. We offer these classes on the 1st Sunday of every month starting at 9am.

Support Groups

United Caregiver Connection Support Group

United Caregiver Connection is a peer support group that meets at Dade City Christian Church at 10 AM, the 3rd Friday of the month. The group is led by Brenda Taggart, a caregiver to her adult twins, living with significant disabilities and Dementia Care Specialist, who works to educate and empower caregivers of those diagnosed with dementia related disorders.

United Caregiver Connection exists to connect caregivers with caregiving tactics, self-care strategies, available resources, and one another. For more information, contact Brenda at (813) 255-9265
