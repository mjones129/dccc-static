You Belong Here.

Connect with God and people at Dade City Christian Church We believe that being connected to a local church should be a positive experience where you have opportunities to grow in your faith and to help others grow in theirs. Whether you’re new to church altogether or if you’re looking for a church to call home, there is a place for you here. Getting connected is simple. Just scroll through this page to learn more.

Join Us This Weekend

No matter where you are on your spiritual journey, we would love for you to attend Dade City Christian. Our hope is that you will be encouraged and inspired when you come. To learn more about our Sunday morning worship experiences, you can plan your visit or visit the About Us page.

Become a Member

There are multiple opportunities to join Dade City Christian Church each month through our Growth Track, where you will Discover God, Develop your faith, and Disciple your circle. To find out more about Growth Track, or to sign up for an upcoming class, visit our Growth Track Page

Join a Life Group

We value the positive change that takes place beyond Sunday morning as we develop meaningful friendships and share life through community. Simply put, we believe life is better together. Our Life Groups are environments where we extend the conversation and encourage one another. To learn more about our Life Groups, or to sign up for a group, you can visit our Life Groups page.
