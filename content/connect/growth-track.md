God has an incredible purpose for your life, and the Growth Track is designed to help you connect with that purpose. Together, we will help you Discover what God has in store for you, Develop your talents and knowledge and unleash you to make Disciples. 

# Discover
We believe that God has created you with a unique purpose in mind. We also believe discovering what that purpose is can be an irritating and long process, so we want to help. 

# Develop
So many people experience church as a weekly event they need to check into for credit. We think this view misses so much of the goodness God has in store for you, and we are committed to helping you grow at your pace, in your place.

# Disciple
We were never meant to sit in a pew and nod our heads. The church has always been meant to live on mission, on purpose. Together, we will lead our community to the one true God, Jesus.
