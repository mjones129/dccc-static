# Join Us This Weekend
No matter where you are on your spiritual journey, we would love for you to attend Dade City Christian. Our hope is that you will be encouraged and inspired when you come. To learn more about our Sunday morning worship experiences, you can [plan your visit](https://people.planningcenteronline.com/forms/78117/fields) or visit the [About Us](/about) page.

# Become a Member
There are multiple opportunities to join Dade City Christian Church each month through our Growth Track, where you will Discover God, Develop your faith, and Disciple your circle. To find out more about Growth Track, or to sign up for an upcoming class, visit our [Growth Track Page](/connect/growth-track)

# Join a Life Group
We value the positive change that takes place beyond Sunday morning as we develop meaningful friendships and share life through community. Simply put, we believe life is better together. Our Life Groups are environments where we extend the conversation and encourage one another. To learn more about our Life Groups, or to sign up for a group, you can visit our [Life Groups](/connect/life-groups) page.
